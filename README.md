#  Nostalgic AI – Historical Journey Generator

An AI-powered Streamlit application that lets users travel through history and experience historical events through immersive storytelling, AI-generated images, and audio narration.

## Live Demo

🔗 https://timebot-lkutzjwwm4qcrcue3gzzbc.streamlit.app/

---

##  Project Overview

Nostalgic AI is an interactive historical exploration platform that transforms historical facts into engaging first-person experiences using Artificial Intelligence.

Users simply enter a historical year and select a perspective such as a journalist, witness, explorer, soldier, or participant. The application then generates a detailed AI-powered narrative, historical imagery, and audio narration to create an immersive journey into the past.

The goal of this project is to make history more engaging, interactive, and accessible through modern AI technologies.

---

##  AI Workflow

1. User enters a historical year.
2. Perplexity AI retrieves historical context.
3. Gemini generates a first-person historical narrative.
4. Stability AI creates historical imagery.
5. gTTS converts the story into audio narration.
6. Streamlit displays the complete historical experience.
